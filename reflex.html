reflex

Press Space to start.
The goal of the game is to get the highest score by pressing down on the marked key. Gain 1 point for every correct keypress but if you press the wrong key or wait too long you will lose 5 points.
The higher the score the shorter the time limit to press the next key will be. Press Space again to stop the game.
Remember the goal is to get the highest score you can, so if you start making mistakes it's better to stop before you ruin your score
